Redbreast sunfish on Lower Flint River basin in SW Georgia. 
   Single Species Multi Season
   Data set used in http://people.oregonstate.edu/~peterjam/occupancy_workshop/hands_on.html

Fishes were collected seasonally (spring and summer) from 2001-2004 in26 streams using a quadrat design, i.e., fishes were collected fromthree 50 - 150 m long segments in each stream via electro fishing.
Are p(t) models within a season sensible? (Why?)Investigate effect of stream flow on local extinction and colonization
