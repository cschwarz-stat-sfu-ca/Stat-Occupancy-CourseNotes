
Coosa Bass
   MultiState Single Season

   http://people.oregonstate.edu/~peterjam/occupancy_workshop/hands_on.html^M
   This example is of Coosa bass collected via electrofishing from four 50-80 m sections
   in streams at 54 sites in the Upper Coosa River basin. The objective was to evaluate
   the effect of streamflow variability on Coosa bass reproduction.
